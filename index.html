About me

Hi! I am a Computer Science PhD student at the MAGNET team at INRIA-Lille, Lille, France under the supervision of Jan Ramon. I finished my previos (equivalent to) Master's degree at Universidad Nacional de Rosario, Argentina. I did master thesis is in the field of High Level Compilation at Universite de Strasbourg under the supervision of Cedric Bastoul and the supervision of the writing by Guillermo Grinblat.

Current Research Interests

I am currently working in Privacy Preserving Machine Learning and Data Mining. My main interest are decentralized computation algorithms and privacy preserving cryptographic tools.
