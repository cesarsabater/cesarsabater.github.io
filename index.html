About Me

I am a Computer Science PhD student at the MAGNET team at INRIA-Lille, France. Under the supervision of Jan Ramon, I am studying how to apply decentralized algorithms and cryptographic techniques to develop privacy preserving machine learning and data mining methods.

I finished my previous (equivalent to) Master's degree at Universidad Nacional de Rosario in 2017. I did my master thesis in the CAMUS team at INRIA and Université de Strasbourg. I worked in the field of high performance computing, specifically with Polyhedral Compilation techniques to dynamically transform programs in execution time. I worked under the supervision of Cédric Bastoul, and the writing supervision of Guillermo Grinblat. You can find the Spanish version of my thesis here and my CV here.

Besides research, I like to swim, run and do kayak when I have the chance.
